Privacy Policy

Last update: May 29, 2024

This Privacy Policy describes the types of personal data we collect about you, how we use your data, how we keep it secure, and the choices you have about your personal data. This Policy applies to all apps developed by Yohan Cassaigne and this website, later referred to as our Service.

By using our Service, you agree to this Privacy Policy and give us an explicit and informed consent to collect and use your personal data in accordance with this Policy. If you do not agree to this Policy, then please do not use our Service.

We reserve the right to change this Policy at any given time. If you want to make sure you are up to date with the latest changes, we advise you to frequently visit this page. Your continued use of our Service will signify your acceptance of the changes made to this Policy.

What part of our Service collects data

Our Service is divided into 2 categories: one that collects data, and one that doesn't.

The category that doesn't collect data is made of this website and the Android versions of the following apps: CoinLuck, Pot3D, and Space Hazards 3D. These apps are not on the homepage because they are not available publicly anymore. They used to collect data but were updated to remove any data collection. As a result, these apps and this website don't collect any data at all.

The category that collects data is made of all the other apps. The next sections of this Policy only apply to this category.

What data we collect

When you use the part of our Service that collects data, some or all of the following types of data will be collected automatically by us or third parties:

  • Your IP address and, as a result, your approximate location.

  • Device identifiers, such as the device’s advertising ID.

  • Crash logs, that may contain information about your device such as your device name, operating system, hardware type, language, and the time and date of your use of our Service.

  • Interaction information such as app launches, taps and video views.

  • Advertising data such as ads you've seen.

  • Performance data such as app launch time, hang rate or energy usage.

  • Gameplay content, such as the scores you earn, the progression of your achievements.

  • Store ratings and reviews you submit.

  • Your purchase history.

Why we collect your data

We use your data for a variety of purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service.

  • To analyze and monitor the use of our Service.

  • To gather valuable information to improve our Service.

  • To detect, prevent and address technical issues.

  • To detect and prevent fraudulent activity.

  • To show personalized ads to you.

Who can access your data

Your data can be accessed by us and others as described in the following situations:

  • Other players and users can see data related to gameplay, such as your scores on leaderboards or your achievements.

  • Everyone can see store reviews you submit as they are public.

  • Our service providers will need information about you to deliver their services to us, so we can provide our own Service. These third parties will generally only access your data in order to provide their services and will not use it for their own purposes.

  • Public authorities may issue a request to us to have access to some of your personal data, in which case we will disclose the necessary information to respond to that request to comply with the laws.

  • Our advertising partner, Admob from Google, will have access to your data to display targeted ads to you. You can read their privacy policy if you want more information about how they use your data.

Security

We value your trust in providing us with your personal information. Thus, we strive to implement the appropriate technical and physical safeguards to ensure your information is protected against unauthorized access, loss, misuse or alteration. Keep in mind though that no method of transmission over the Internet, or method of storage, is 100% secure and reliable.

Your opt-out rights

As a user, there are various ways you can prevent the collection of some or all types of personal data. For data related to gameplay, you may choose to disable some functionalities of our Service, such as Game Center and Google Play Games, by not signing in to those services. However, note that you wouldn’t be taking advantage of the full potential of our Service.

You also have the possibility to opt out of targeted advertising. On iOS, you can simply disable tracking permissions on our apps. On Android, a setting should allow you to opt out of interest-based ads.

Finally, by uninstalling our apps from your devices, you automatically stop the collection of your personal data. This will not prevent your previously collected data from being used as described in the previous sections. However, you have the right to ask us to delete your data. If you wish to have your data deleted, simply contact us and we will make sure to satisfy your request.

Note that we have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party websites.

Children’s privacy

Our Service is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under that age. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we immediately take the necessary actions to delete this information. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, you can contact us so we can delete it. And if you are a child under the age of 13, please do not use our Service and do not send any information to us.
