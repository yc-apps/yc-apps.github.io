Space Hazards 3D

This app is a 3D game that allows you to control a spaceship crossing a field of asteroids. These asteroids represent the “space hazards” that you will be confronted to. Hitting them will make you lose a life every time. The game is over when you lose all your lives.

As you try to survive through this field of asteroids, your goal is to reach a score as high as possible. Your score increases every second automatically and when you collect coins and diamonds. You will also find hearts that add extra lives, and bonuses that improve your chances of survival or help you increase your score.

This game also works with Google Play Games on Android and Game Center on iOS to enhance your gaming experience. Thus, your scores are automatically sent to a leaderboard that lets you compare yourself to your friends and other players. There are also achievements that you can try to complete.
