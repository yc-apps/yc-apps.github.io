Solviks

Have you ever wondered how to solve the Rubik's Cube? Do you want to become a pro at it? If that’s the case, this app is for you!

This app is made of a game, a solver and tutorials in the same place.

The game part lets you solve a cube of various possible sizes in order to practice your skills and improve your performance. Stats are also available to track your performance. Note that different styles of cubes also exist to make the game even more fun.

Moreover, the game works with Google Play Games on Android and Game Center on iOS. When playing, a score is given to you, depending on the cube's difficulty and your performance, to compare yourself to other players on a leaderboard. Achievements are also available to let you challenge yourself.

The solver part allows you to solve a cube of size 2 or size 3 with the smallest amount of moves. Just enter the colors that constitute your cube’s configuration and let the solver guide you with animations showing you the moves to apply. A camera scanner is also available to let you scan your cube’s faces directly.

The final part of this app is tutorials. Tutorials are here to teach you how to solve the cube of size 2 or size 3. They will guide you step by step and will show you how to solve each layer of the cubes. Note that these tutorials are also available during the game phase so you can find help while practicing your skills.
